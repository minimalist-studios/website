Privacy Policy


Effective 16 June 2022
Last Updated 16 June 2022

Minimalist Studios’s mission is to connect the world through games. We deliver fun, social entertainment experiences to people around the world. In pursuit of our mission, we collect, use, store, disclose and otherwise process (“process”) certain information from our players. Being transparent about the processing of players’ information and respecting their privacy is incredibly important to Minimalist Studios.

This Privacy Policy applies when your personal information is processed as part of our Services (described below). Please read it because it will tell you how we process, your personal information when providing these Services to you. This Privacy Policy is not, however, a contract and does not create any legal rights or obligations.

If you have questions, comments, or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or practices, please Contact Us.

Minimalist Studios offers a variety of online and mobile games. Our Privacy Policy applies whenever your personal information is processed as part of our “Services,” which means our games, products, services, content, Minimalist Studios.com, and/or other domains or websites operated by Minimalist Studios. It describes how we process your personal information and your rights and choices concerning your personal information.

Minimalist Studios develops and publishes games for web, mobile devices and other platforms on which players can use our Services. You can find a list of companies controlled by Minimalist Studios Inc. here. These are referred to as our “Group Companies”. “Minimalist Studios,” “we,” “our,” or “us” in this Privacy Policy refers to those Group Companies that operate under this Privacy Policy.

For purposes of data protection laws, Minimalist Studios Inc. is the “data controller” of your personal information. We will refer to countries in the European Economic Area (EEA), Switzerland and the UK collectively as the “European Countries” in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy applies whenever your personal information is processed as part of our “Services,” which means our games, products, services, content, Minimalist Studios.com, and/or other domains or websites operated by Minimalist Studios. It describes how we process your personal information when providing these Services to you, and your rights and choices concerning your personal information. The Privacy Policy’s sections are listed and hyperlinked in the Table of Contents above for easier navigation.

If you play Minimalist Studios’s games or use Minimalist Studios’s other Services, your information will be processed in the ways described in this Privacy Policy.

When you use our Services, we will collect personal information about the ways in which you use and interact with our Services, such as when and for how long you play our games, which game features and other players you interact with, any purchases you make, what progress or levels you achieve, and other gameplay activities in which you participate. Minimalist Studios’s Terms of Service explain our policies and terms related to charges, billing, third-party credits, and virtual currencies.
